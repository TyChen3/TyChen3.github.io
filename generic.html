About Me

University of Waterloo Engineering Student | 3D Designer | Entrepreneur

My Engineering Journey

I am an undergraduate engineering student at the University of Waterloo with a strong foundation in mechanical design, manufacturing, and technical problem-solving. My professional experience includes mechanical design at Waddell Engineering Ltd., where I focused on fabrication and assembly drafting, and validation testing at Bend All Automotive ULC. Recently, I have also expanded my technical toolkit into software development, building custom applications that range from document formatting tools to technical feedback platforms for audio mixing.

Prototyping & Entrepreneurship

I believe in bringing concepts off the screen and into the physical world. I operate a premium 3D printing service utilizing high-speed Bambu Lab technology. Managing this setup gives me a practical, hands-on understanding of manufacturing tolerances, real-world material applications, and rapid prototyping capabilities.

Alongside my engineering work, I manage an online retail business within the Trading Card Game space. I design, source, and distribute custom UV-resistant acrylic display cases for sealed products. Managing this business has sharpened my skills in overseas supplier logistics, bulk inventory management, and client communication.

Beyond the Screen

When I step away from CAD environments and code editors, I stay highly active. I am a Second Degree Black Belt in Meibukan Goju-Ryu Karate, having trained for over a decade. I am also an avid indoor rock climber—bouldering around the V5 level—and enjoy participating in local events like the Brawl in the Fall climbing competition.

As a creative outlet, I produce music using FL Studio, which allows me to blend creative composition with the highly technical aspects of audio engineering and energy mapping.
